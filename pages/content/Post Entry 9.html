Grey Hats in Hacking: Ethical Hackers with a Blend of Black Hat and White Hat Activities

Grey hats in hacking refer to individuals who engage in hacking activities with a combination of both black hat and white hat practices.

They may sometimes violate laws or ethical standards, but their intentions are not typically malicious like black hat hackers.

Grey hat hackers often look for vulnerabilities in systems without the owner's permission, but they may also use their skills to help improve security by reporting these vulnerabilities to the system owners.

While their actions may be questionable, grey hat hackers do not have the same malicious intent as black hat hackers.

It is important to note that hiring a grey hat hacker is illegal, as their activities involve unauthorized access to systems.
